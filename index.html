What Makes it Different?

Incredible Energy

Ecstatic Zouk Fusion parties are the ideal dance parties. Beautiful movements and structure from Zouk allow people to feel powerful in their bodies while freedom from Ecstatic and Fusion dance lets people express themselves. Ecstatic Zouk Fusion is the easiest and most fun dance style to learn. It can be done on any floor too, making it perfect for any venu, from dance halls to bars and yoga studios

Awesome Connection

Ecstatic Zouk Fusion makes connection effortless. Partner dancing raises the energy of the party further than many have ever seen at events where people primarily dance alone. The way Ecstatic Zouk Fusion is taught teaches people to feel the energy and trust themselves in a way that makes everyone feel very safe and very easily able to drop into themselves and the movement. The energy of the dance itself makes it perfect for events with or without alcohol and is sure to please all attendees.


Meet Mason


Mason has been teaching and hosting dance events for the last 4 years and is also a yoga teacher, martial arts instructor, DJ, and musician. Mason created Ecstatic Zouk Fusion as a way to celebrate the beautiful body movements and masculine-feminine polar energy of Zouk while allowing for the freedom of expression of Ecstatic and Fusion dance.

Mason's workshops start with a warmup lesson that teaches people how to dance this style while also getting them in the zone. The focus on energy, connection, and creativity allows people to find their own style and learn far faster than in a more traditional dance setting. This is followed by 2-3 hours of dancing and socializing which people remember for the rest of their lives.
